Voices from the Engine:
A Digital Collection of Migrant Worker Poetry

With the help of the Mellon-Mays Undergraduate Fellowship, I have researched and translated Chinese migrant worker poetry over the last year and a half.

This digital collection contains a total of fourty four poems, and I provide close readings of every poem to assist the reader in understanding this complex poetry.

As these migrant workers give voice to the human rights issues they face in modern China, I find it important to translate their works and amplify their voice to as wide of an audience as possible.
